POEMS OF THOUGHTS

DEEP IN THOUGHTS;

Taking a long journey into the imagination,
On the path of 'maybe and assumptions,
With both dimmed and lightened phases of assumptions,
I get deep in thoughts.

To another world, I belong,
As my meditation is strong,
Getting to muse so long,
Contemplating whether my presumption isn't wrong,
Because assurance isn't among.

Like what am I thinking?
Assuming if yesteryears should be today,
And histories can get a play,
So I'm still on the way,
Because these thoughts have no boundary.

Mind, brain, and nature,
They're the heroes that explore,
For my body is just an expenditure.
The brain yearning for my composure,
While others are trying to make a treasure.

After all the randoms,
My body now gets its freedom,
Because this trance of thoughts might be boredom,
But can also turn out to be a moment of wisdom,
As it freezes my body seldom.

Not a psych,
But when I get deep in my thoughts,
It always leads me to a journey in my trance.

This poem was written by Ahmad Bashir Siraj

DEEP IN THOUGHTS 2;

Deep in thoughts,
Preoccupied deep down in the soul,
Consumed under silence and sighs,
For it's a conference between the mind and the heart.

Lots I think,
On the horizon, I focused
Illuminating my imagination,
With assumptions and comparatives,
My perception becomes widen.

Deep in me, I feel the conversation,
The mind talks through the mouth,
And the brain demonstrates with hands,
Stars of conception going around the head,
Because profound thoughts cannot be hidden.

So I realized;
I'm just mumbling alone
Chatting myself; A psych, no I'm not.
It's precisely a fascination with deep thoughts.
